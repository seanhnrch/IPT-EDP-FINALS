Attebdance Monitoring System

BDO Unibank, Inc. is one of the largest banks in the Philippines, providing a wide range of financial products and services including savings and checking accounts, loans, investment services, insurance, and corporate banking solutions. The Calumpit, Bulacan branch serves local residents, small and medium enterprises, and corporate clients within the municipality and nearby communities.
The branch operates under the corporate policies and compliance standards of BDO, ensuring high levels of operational efficiency, security, and customer service excellence.

The primary objectives of the Attendance Monitoring System are:
●	To automate attendance recording and management.
●	To reduce errors associated with manual attendance systems.
●	To prevent fraudulent attendance marking.
●	To provide real-time monitoring and reporting features.
●	To maintain
●	 a centralized database for secure storage and retrieval of attendance data.
The system will implement role-based access control (RBAC) to ensure security and accountability.
